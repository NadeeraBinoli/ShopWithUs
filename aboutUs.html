How we Started!

ShopWithUs Store is a family-owned and operated flower shop that has been serving the community for over 10 years. We are proud to offer a wide selection of fresh flowers, plants, and dried flowers for any occasion.


Our story began in 2012 when Miss.Nadeera Binoli founder of ShopWithUs Store opened the first shop in Kiridiwela Sri Lanka. Miss.Nadeera Binoli was a passionate gardener and floral designer, and she dreamed of creating a shop where people could come to find the perfect flowers for any occasion.


Over the years, ShopWithUsStore has grown to become one of the most respected flower shops in the area. We are known for our high-quality flowers, creative arrangements, and friendly customer service. We are committed to providing our customers with the best possible experience.


We are also proud to be a member of the local community. We support local schools, charities, and businesses. We also offer a variety of educational workshops and events throughout the year.


We invite you to visit our shop or website to learn more about our products and services. We look forward to helping you find the perfect flowers for any occasion.


Hello I'm Nadeera Binoli.

I opened ShopWithUs Store because I have a passion for flowers and I love helping people to express their emotions through them. Flowers are a beautiful and meaningful way to show someone you care, and I love being able to help people to find the perfect flowers for any occasion.


I also wanted to create a business that would make a positive impact on the community. I support local schools and charities, and I hire employees from the local community. I also offer a variety of educational workshops and events throughout the year.


I am committed to providing my customers with the best possible experience. I offer a wide variety of fresh flowers, plants, and gifts, and I have a team of experienced florists who are passionate about their work. I also offer a variety of services, such as fresh flower delivery, custom floral arrangements, and gift baskets.


I am proud to be a part of the local community, and I am grateful for the opportunity to serve my customers. I love seeing the smiles on their faces when they receive their flowers, and I know that I am helping them to make special moments even more memorable.
